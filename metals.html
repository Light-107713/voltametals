STRATEGIC & RARE EARTH METALS

Also known as critical metals, technology metals, or minor metals, these are elements that are necessary for technological and industrial processes but are in short supply and have no known alternatives.

Rare Earth Metals

A set of 17 nearly- indistinguishable lustrous silvery-white soft heavy metals. Compounds containing rare earth have diverse applications in electrical and electronic components, lasers, glass, magnetic materials, and industrial processes.

Metal Ore

Metal Ore is extracted from the earth through and treated or often via, to extract the valuable metals or minerals. The grade of ore refers to the concentration of the desired material it contains.
